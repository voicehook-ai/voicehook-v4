voicehook.ai — what's happening here?

Talk to AI: Live voice call with our voice agent (STT → LLM → TTS over wss://rtc.voicehook.ai). Hit the phone icon and start talking. Each turn lands in the transcript below.

Speech / Music mode: Speech is the AI-call mode. Music turns the dome into a visualizer that reacts to your mic (or whatever is playing in the tab).

Invite an agent: You get an invite snippet — paste it into another LLM session (Claude Code, Cursor, ZeroClaw, Hermes). The external agent joins as a second brain and can push TTS replies through your call.

Presence chips (top): grey = not in room · teal = joined · green pulse = speaking right now.

Transcript: Hit ≡ at the bottom or press Esc for fullscreen. Wheel-scroll works inside the transcript; outside it zooms the dome.

Agent CLI: uvx voicehook-agent join <url> · Skill doc: /agent/SKILL.md
